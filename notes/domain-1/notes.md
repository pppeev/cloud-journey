Lesson 1.3B prof. Messer - Mobile Device Management 
MDM - Mobile Device Management, sets polices on what is allowed or not allowed, centralized control over the phone
BYOD - Bring your own device, ex. bringing your own phone to work somewhere is “BYOD” and MDM is them setting up a specialized software, which regulates what the device you have brought does and doesn’t do, MDM can be used not only for your device but for devices they have given you (COPE-Corporate owned, personally enabled)
CYOD - (Choose Your Own Device), employees choose a device from a company list and the company provides it, and they have full control over the device.
